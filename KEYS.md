### Modifiers

List of modifiers:

    "fn"

    "cmd"
    "lcmd"
    "rcmd"

    "shift"
    "lshift"
    "rshift"

    "alt"
    "lalt"
    "ralt"

    "ctrl"
    "lctrl"
    "rctrl"

    "hyper" (cmd + shift + alt + ctrl)

    "meh" (shift + alt + ctrl)
